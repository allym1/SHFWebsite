About

La Société Honoraire de Français, otherwise known as the French Honor Society, is an organization that fosters the love of French and francophone culture. La Société Honoraire de Français is supported by the American Association of Teachers of French, or AATF. This 2018-2019 year, the SHF of DPHS will hold monthly meetings in H-23 in Madame Lent's room. Activities at these meetings will include: watching French movies and videos, eating food from francophone countries, learning about current events in francophone countires, and listening to French music. The goal of la Société Honoraire de Français is to celebrate French and francophone culture, and to recognize students with outstanding scholarship in the study of the French language.

Membership Benefits

  • invitation to monthly meetings
  • leadership opportunities to serve as officers
  • opportunitiy to compete in French competitions
  • the placement of a special seal on the graduate's diploma
  • the wearing of a tri-colored cord at graduation
  • the right to wear the official emblem/pin of the honor society

How to join

Membership Requirements

  • Maintained an A-(minus) average or higher in French during the semester of selection and averaged a cumulative grade of A-(minus) or higher for all of French work awarded secondary school credit.
  • Maintained a B-(minus) average or higher in all other subjects during the semester of selection and an averaged cumulative grade of B-(minus) or higher in all other subjects.
  • Registered for each annual National French Contest (Le Grand Concours) as of 2018.
  • Is a junior or senior in high school.
